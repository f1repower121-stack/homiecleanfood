### 1. Smart Recommendation Engine
```
Algorithm: Personalized Meal Suggestions

Input:
├─ Customer history (top-rated meals)
├─ Dietary preferences (allergies, meat pref)
├─ Nutrition goals (calories, macros)
├─ Current streak (frequency adjust)
└─ Seasonal ingredients (Thai local)

Logic:
1. Calculate similarity score (0-100%) for each meal
   - Taste match: How similar to top meals?
   - Nutrition match: Hits macro goals?
   - Preference match: Respects allergies?
   - Novelty score: Not eaten in 30 days?

2. Weight by factors:
   - History match: 40% (they like it)
   - Nutrition match: 35% (hits goals)
   - Variety: 15% (don't repeat)
   - Streak factor: 10% (keep it interesting)

3. Return top 3 recommendations with:
   - Match percentage
   - Why recommended
   - Nutrition breakdown
   - Customer rating
```
